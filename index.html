Jonathan Polk

I am a Professor in the Department of Political Science at Lund University.

My research focuses on political parties, intra-party politics, and representation in European democracies.

I teach courses and supervise student work on comparative politics, European Union politics, and quantitative methods.

My research has been published in Comparative Political Studies, European Journal of Political Research, European Union Politics, Journal of European Public Policy, Journal of Politics, and Party Politics, among other outlets.

More information about my publications can be found on Google Scholar
