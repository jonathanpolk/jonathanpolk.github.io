Jonathan Polk

I am an Associate Professor at Lund University.

My research focuses on political parties, intra-party politics, and representation in European democracies.

I teach courses and supervise student work on comparative politics and European Union politics.

My research has been published in Comparative Political Studies, European Journal of Political Research, European Union Politics, Party Politics, and Journal of Politics, among other outlets.
